About Spicyy

Welcome to Spicyy – a restaurant where great food goes hand in hand with sustainability. The idea of Spicyy was born out of a desire to serve great food without compromising sustainability. We aim to minimise the amount of waste we produce with everyday actions that follow our sustainable values and waste-free ideology. We offer a wide range of delicacies from all over Bangldesh with over two to ten years of experience. Over the years, tandoori dishes, darts and masalas have become especially popular with visitors. Friendly and fast service guarantees a successful taste experience. In the evenings, the romantic atmosphere of Spicyy attracts visitors to spend an atmospheric evening by candlelight. The interior is invested in elegance. Solid wood furniture and eye-catching Bangladeshi decorations create an authentic Bangladeshi atmosphere for an evening out. Many groups will come to enjoy Spicyy’s excellent food and the restaurant’s unique atmosphere.

We also organize private events

We deliver food for private events (min. 25 people).
